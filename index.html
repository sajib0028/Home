Our Working Process

Sign up

All you need is a Phone number. You can request the Mobile repair van or Tow truck from the LIVEBREAKDOWNS mobile app.

Create Ticket

Creating service ticket is an easy-to-use self guided process. Click I need assistance button, fill in the details about breakdown and Submit to broadcast your service ticket to get matched with near by help.

Assign Ticket

Go to the ticket details page and click Assign to the mechanic you selected. Full ticket details are auto-saved for future references.

Give Reviews

Go to ticket details page and close the ticket. It will ask you to add rating. Rate the mechanic to help build their service profile.
